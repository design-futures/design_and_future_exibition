Sustainable thinking is a realization that the resources we have now are not going to last forever and that we need to change what we are doing so we will be able to continue living the way we are now. Sustainable thinking is having the constant awareness of trying not to live so wastefully and being conscious of what we are taking from the environment and what we are putting back into it and whether our actions are harmful or not. I also think being sustainable is a balancing act. It works like a give and take.
Leather in this case: though being a natural material was never termed sustainable, it always had these pros and cons that we have been discussing and various betterments have been suggested. This prompted me to research the existing alternatives and how far they are feasible. Discussing ideas made me realize that there are many ways of exploring a particular material.
