What was the origin of your enquiry? Where are you coming from on, what motivated you to take up this exploration? What are your philosophies around the idea? What motivated you towards this? What were some assumptions and notions you had? What was the idea or thought you came across?

Initially I had started with working on IOT and automation and was thinking about coming up with some kind of product using it. But then after going through the reviews by my peers and faculty I realised that I could work in a completely different direction where I could use speculative design to come up with an alternative world. I was particularly inspired by the work that Superflex had done in the field of speculative design. I also decided to use speculative design for my enquiry and hence came up with a concept where people live on the surface and inside water.



Did any author or fiction or philosophy inspire you? How did you go about exploring the inquiry you were interested in? How did you document the info you were coming across? How did you devise or plan an approach? Did you crush or prove any assumptions? How did you perform research?

While I was doing my research bit I came across a video that said - Is finding life on other planets the solution or we could simply start living on water. This sentence was my main inspiration to work in this direction. I also saw some amazing work that was done on floating cities by Oceanix. I read about how much work had been done on under water architectures and came across many amazing ideas and kept on making notes about things I felt interested me the most. So then instead of coming up with a complete plan for a city i decided to break it into different components like power, transportation etc. and came up with ideas and maps for each and at the end put it all together to come up with a very basic plan for how the city might look.

I wanted to work on the concept of how the world on water and under water would look what kind of structures might exist and made a few sketches. I am working on the idea though it is a far fetched idea like how things are going about.
